Getting Started

Logging In

Access Client Approval & Sign-Off Management System by navigating to your application URL and entering your credentials:

your-email@company.com
••••••••

Forgot Password?

Click "Forgot your password?" on the login page. You'll receive a password reset link via email.

First Login

After logging in for the first time, you'll see:

  • Dashboard: Overview of system statistics and recent activity
  • Navigation Sidebar: Access to all modules based on your role
  • User Menu: Profile settings and logout option (top-right)

Managing Clients

Clients are the core of your approval management. Here's how to manage them:

Adding a New Client

  1. 1 Click "Clients" in the sidebar navigation
  2. 2 Click the "Add Client" button (top-right)
  3. 3 Fill in the client information form
  4. 4 Click "Create Client" to save

Viewing & Editing Clients

Client List Features:

  • Search: Find clients by name, email, or company
  • Filter: View active or inactive clients only
  • Sort: Click column headers to sort (name, email, date)
  • Pagination: Navigate through pages if you have many clients

Client Actions:

View Details

Click client name or eye icon to see full details

Edit Client

Click pencil icon to modify client information

Delete Client

Click trash icon (requires confirmation)

Warning: Deleting Clients

Deleting a client cannot be undone. All associated approval requests will also be affected. Consider setting the status to "Inactive" instead.

Managing Client Status

Instead of deleting clients, you can toggle their status:

Active
  • Client appears in lists
  • Can create approval requests
  • Receives email notifications
Inactive
  • Hidden by default (can filter to view)
  • Cannot create new approvals
  • Data preserved for records

Managing Approval Requests

Approval requests are the heart of Client Approval & Sign-Off Management System. Learn how to create and manage them:

Creating an Approval Request

  1. 1 Navigate to "Approvals" from the sidebar
  2. 2 Click "Create Approval" button
  3. 3 Fill in the approval request form
  4. 4 Upload attachments (optional)
  5. 5 Click "Create Approval Request"

Understanding Priority Levels

Low

Non-urgent items, flexible timeline

Medium

Standard approvals, normal priority

High

Important, needs prompt attention

Urgent

Critical, immediate action required

Viewing & Filtering Approvals

Filter by Status:

Click any status button to filter the approval list

Approval Actions

Approving a Request

  1. 1. Click on the approval request to view details
  2. 2. Review all information and attachments
  3. 3. Click the "Approve" button
  4. 4. Optionally add an approval message/note
  5. 5. Confirm the approval

Email Notification: The client will automatically receive an approval email (if email is configured).

Rejecting a Request

  1. 1. Click on the approval request to view details
  2. 2. Click the "Reject" button
  3. 3. Add a rejection reason/message (recommended)
  4. 4. Confirm the rejection

Best Practice: Always provide a clear reason for rejection to help clients understand what needs to be changed.

Editing a Request

You can edit pending approval requests:

  • Update title, description, or priority
  • Change due date
  • Add or remove attachments
  • Cannot edit approved/rejected requests

Profile Management

Accessing Your Profile

Click your name in the top-right corner, then select "Profile".

Update Profile Information

  • Change your name
  • Update email address
  • Click "Update Profile" to save

Change Password

  • Enter current password
  • Enter new password (min 8 chars)
  • Confirm new password
  • Click "Update Password"

Security Tip

Use a strong password with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Change your password regularly for better security.

Tips & Best Practices

Client Management

  • Keep client information up-to-date
  • Use unique, valid email addresses
  • Set inactive instead of deleting
  • Use company field for better organization

Approval Requests

  • Write clear, descriptive titles
  • Provide detailed descriptions
  • Set appropriate priority levels
  • Attach relevant files/documents

Workflow Efficiency

  • Review dashboard daily
  • Act on pending approvals promptly
  • Use filters to focus on priorities
  • Set realistic due dates

Communication

  • Always add notes when approving/rejecting
  • Be specific about rejection reasons
  • Keep clients informed of progress
  • Respond to urgent items quickly
